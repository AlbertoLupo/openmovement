BuildAX

Environment Sensors

Monitor your environment

It can be an uphill struggle for tenants to demonstrate to their landlords that their homes need improvement.

BuildAX is an internet-connected electronic sensor kit which can provide evidence when poor building conditions are causing problems such as damp in the home or workplace. As well as monitoring temperature, humidity and light levels, each BuildAX sensor includes a motion detector, and can be mounted on a door or window to detect when that fitting is opened.

If you own a computer, you can use it to view the data collected by the system securely in real time - however, a computer is not required to participate.

How to get involved

We are currently looking for volunteers to trial the system in homes and businesses, in Newcastle and further afield.

BuildAX is under development at Newcastle University's Culture Lab. If you would be interested in participating in a (free) pilot program, email us at buildax@ncl.ac.uk or contact Samuel Finnigan on (0191) 208 4624 for more information.
